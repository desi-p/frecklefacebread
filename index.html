About Us

Hi, my name is Sarah, I am a wife, mom, homeschool teacher and now a Baker. My journey into baking began in November of 2021 when a sweet friend gave me some sourdough starter. Overnight, it seems, I went from not being able to bake cookies well to baking various types of sourdough bread. It is something I have truly enjoyed. I have been able to walk this journey with my family by my side. When I am not baking you can find me going to basketball games for my kids or eating at some local spot around town. All the bread we make is sourdough based breads, from artisan boule to sandwich bread. Sometimes we add flavors to them, sometimes we keep it simple but it's always delicious. Join this journey and enjoy some homemade bread.
